Down the Path Untravelled

Feeling adventurous, Lola ignored the pit in her stomach and started towards the foreign path. As she was moving through the thick brush and branches, she noticed the outline of a person limping just a few feet ahead. She called out to the figure “Hey! Are you alright?” She waited a few seconds for the person to reply but got no response.

She assumed the person did not hear her, so she lightly jogged ahead to catch up to them. She was a few paces behind the mysterious person when they turned around to faced her. The person appeared to be a middle-aged man wearing clothing that was torn and bloodstained. Shocked by his appearance, Lola stopped dead in her tracks. Within seconds the man disappeared before her very eyes.
